Best Builds

Optimize your Z-Team with these recommended builds. Each hero has a clear stat priority that maximizes their strengths and covers their weaknesses. Use this as your dispatch cheat sheet.

Invisigal

Mobility + Combat
Focus:Mobility (5★), Combat (4★)
Weakness:Vigor (2★)

Prioritize Mobility and Combat upgrades. Invest spare points into Intellect to unlock hacking and puzzle-solving options in missions, making her more versatile beyond pure stealth.

Flambae

Combat + Mobility
Focus:Combat (5★), Mobility (4★)
Weakness:Charisma (2★)

Max out Combat first — Flambae is the team's primary damage dealer. Invest in Mobility to close gaps and chase down fleeing targets. Secondary focus on Vigor to improve survivability in extended fights.

Malevola

Combat + Charisma
Focus:Combat (4★), Charisma (4★)
Weakness:Vigor (3★)

Balance Combat and Charisma upgrades. This makes Malevola a hybrid who can fight and talk. Her well-rounded nature means you can deploy her into nearly any mission type without worrying about a hard counter.

Sonar

Intellect + Charisma (base); Combat + Vigor (Mega Bat)
Focus:Intellect (4★), Charisma (4★)
Weakness:Vigor (3★)

In base form, Sonar is your detective and face-man. Invest in Intellect for puzzles and hacking, Charisma for dialogue. When Mega Bat triggers, Combat becomes 4★ and Vigor becomes 4★ — making him a capable fighter. Learn mission triggers to time transformations optimally.

Blonde Blazer

Adaptable — fill any role
Focus:Combat (4★), Intellect (4★)
Weakness:Vigor (4★)

Blonde Blazer needs no specific build direction — she's already excellent everywhere. Use her to fill whatever gap your team has. Pair her with specialists and let her cover their weaknesses. The most forgiving hero for new players.

Phenomaman

Vigor + Mobility + Intellect
Focus:Vigor (5★), Intellect (4★)
Weakness:Combat (2★)

Max out Vigor first to become nearly unkillable. Invest in Mobility for repositioning and rescue speed, Intellect for puzzle missions. Her Combat will always be low — always pair her with a damage-dealer.

# Team Composition Tips

The Balanced Squad

One well-rounded hero core + two specialists to cover mission types.

Blonde Blazer + Flambae + Sonar

The Synergy Squad

Exploit the game's strongest duo pairings for maximum effect.

Malevola + Sonar + Invisigal

The Heavy Hitters

Pure combat focus — overwhelm enemies before they can act.

Flambae + Invisigal + Phenomaman

The Diplomatic Squad

Talk your way through anything. Minimal combat required.

Malevola + Sonar + Blonde Blazer
