# FAQ

What makes Blonde Blazer special?

She's the only hero with straight 4★ across all five stats — zero weaknesses. No other Z-Team member can be deployed into literally any mission type without risk. She's not the best at anything, but she's never the wrong choice.

Is Blonde Blazer good for beginners?

Perfect for beginners. She's the most forgiving hero — you can't make a bad decision with her. Use her to learn mission types while you figure out which specialists you prefer. Veterans still value her as the ultimate flex pick.

Who should I pair with Blonde Blazer?

Anyone. Literally. She fills whatever gap your team has. Pair her with Flambae if you need the damage dealer to have backup. Pair her with Phenomaman if you want the tank to have offensive support. She's the glue.

Does Blonde Blazer have any weaknesses?

Her only weakness is lacking a 5★ specialization. In missions that demand max Combat (5★), Flambae outperforms her. In max Charisma situations, specialized Malevola/Sonar edge her out. She's the master of none.
